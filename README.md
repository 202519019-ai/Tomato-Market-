## 🌟 Project Overview

The **Tomato Market Intelligence System** is a data-driven web application developed using **Python**, **Streamlit**, and **Plotly** to support tomato market analysis across India.

The system transforms historical mandi market data into interactive dashboards that help farmers, researchers, traders, and policymakers understand market behavior, identify profitable selling opportunities, and make informed decisions.

The application combines market intelligence, statistical analysis, forecasting, and visualization in a single platform.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze historical tomato market prices across India.
- Monitor market arrivals and price fluctuations.
- Compare prices across states and mandis.
- Forecast future market trends.
- Identify profitable selling markets.
- Support farmer decision-making using data analytics.
